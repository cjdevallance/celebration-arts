Donate to Celebration Arts

Celebration Arts is deeply appreciative of those who share our commitment to providing training and performance opportunities and a community that celebrates the human spirit. Every gift makes a difference

Throughout the year, there are many opportunities for you, your organization, business or corporation to be directly involved in, and receive recognition in conjunction with our performances and programs. Your tax deductible contributions will support the training and development efforts and the costs of staging productions.

Workplace Giving

As a non-profit organization, Celebration Arts can benefit through your participation in workplace giving programs. Please confer with your employer's human resources office for information. These programs may offer payroll deduction opportunities. Others may provide an employer match of your contribution. Whatever the process or method, Celebration Arts appreciates your thoughtfulness.

Planned Giving

You can be a benefactor of Celebration Arts, helping to reach further into the community for talent and audiences by including it in your will. In addition to bequests, there are many planned gifts, which can benefit the association while at the same time providing you advantage in managing your Tax liability. Among these gifts are life insurance, real estate and charitable remainder trusts.
